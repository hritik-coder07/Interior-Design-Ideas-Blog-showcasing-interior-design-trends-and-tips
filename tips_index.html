Interior Design Tips

1. Start with a Plan

Before diving into decorating, make a plan. Assess your space, set a budget, and outline your style goals. This roadmap will help guide your choices and keep you focused as you create a cohesive design.

2. Embrace Neutral Bases

Use neutral colors as the foundation of your space. Light grays, beiges, and whites create a clean, calming environment and serve as versatile backdrops. You can then add personality with colorful accents or textures.

3. Balance Function and Aesthetics

Ensure your space is both beautiful and practical. Choose furniture that fits your lifestyle and consider how each piece will be used.

4. Use the Rule of Three

Group decor items in sets of three for a balanced, visually pleasing look. Vary heights and sizes to create depth and interest on surfaces.

5. Incorporate Natural Elements

Bring the outdoors in with natural elements like plants, wooden furniture, and stone accents. Greenery and organic textures can make a space feel warm and inviting.
